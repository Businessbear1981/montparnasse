In the meantime, a number of research and expertise studies were carried out - an extensive material survey was carried out 
by ATS MENSURAE in Rome, which was essential. The results of the expertise were compiled in his review by art historian and 
expert Jiri Verner, who confirmed Modigliani's authorship, but there are some ambiguities or inaccuracies in his review - he 
writes about signature and dating at the bottom left, where I find nothing, while a clear signature without date is at the bottom 
right. He writes about the date 1907, which I do not see in the painting, and further due to other circumstances I believe that 
this date is impossible. 
Although Modigliani was already in Paris during that year but being there only for one year and during that time, he was not 
yet well-versed enough to be able to arrange models in order to paint nudes, the painting is very similar to the act of Kiki de 
Montparnasse by Modigliani's close friend, with whom he lived since 1913, in the same house on Montparnasse. There are 
obvious similarities in the paintings of both painters. Modigliani even backed away from his distinctive style and leaned toward 
Kisling's style. It can be assumed from this, that the painters painted the paintings at the same time. In 1907 Kisling was not yet 
in Paris at all and was 16 years old at this time. 
Furthermore, model Kiki de Montparnasse, real name Alice Ernestine Prin, was 5-6 years old in 1907. And the proof 
that this is a depiction of this model is evidenced not only by a formal comparison of her portraits, but also by the 
authentication of Kisling's painting, where the model's name bears the name of the painting.

The Italian painter and sculptor Amedeo Modigliani was born on July 12, 1884, in Livorno, Tuscany, and died on January 25, 
1920, in Paris, at the age of just 35 years old, shortly before his planned wedding with his girlfriend Jeanne Hébuterne, with 
whom he had a child. She committed suicide on the day of his funeral, at the age of 21. 
Modigliani came from a Jewish family, his father was a banker, but he died early and then the family had material problems, 
Modigliani wasn’t healthy from early childhood and very young, contracted tuberculosis. 
From 1902 he studied painting at the Academy in Florence and then in Venice, and in 1906 he relocated to Paris already 
suffering from tuberculosis, that time he was 22 years old. 
In Paris, he settled in the art district of Montparnasse and his close friendsbecame a number of artists, especially 
later Moise Kisling, then Pablo Picasso - with whom, however, they were considered the main artistic rivals. Modigliani worked 
here until his death.

The Polish-French Jewish painter Moise Kisling or Mojzesz Kisling was born on January 22, 1891, in Krakow and died on April 
29, 1953. He came to Paris in 1910 and in 1915 acquired French citizenship. First he came to Montmartre in 1910, during that 
time it was a famous place for artists, shortly after - probably in 1913 he moved to the southern hill of Paris - Montparnasse, 
where it was cheaper living, and for this reason many artists moved there. In Montparnasse, he lived in the same house with 
Modigliani, which strengthened their friendship.

The name Montparnasse originated in the 18th century and was used to describe a desolate hill, located south of Paris. The 
name referred to Greek mythology, Mount Parnassus. But the place was desolate and artistic life was primarily

The World's Fair in 1889 and the rich artistic life of Paris attracted more and more artists from all over the world who were 
finding there the cheapest place to live in Paris, in the suburb of Montparnasse, which had been part of Paris since 1860.

Just as Montmartrehad a muse a generation or two generations earlier, was a model and artist depicted by all artists 
- La Goulue, real name Louise Weber (12.7.1866-29.1.1929), so Kiki de Montparnasse became a popular model and artist on 
Montparnasse, her real name was Alice Ernestine Prin (2.10.1901-29.4.1953).
As an illegitimate child, she lived in poverty and was cared for by her grandmother. At the age of 12, she was sent 
from a small rural town to Paris to live with her mother and to find work there. At first, she worked in shops and bakeries and at 
the age of 14 she began to become a model sculptor. She was also active as a painter and later became a model to many artists. 
One of the most famous paintings is Nu assis by Moise Kisling. 
Moise Kisling painted Kiki many times, and later, upon Modigliani'sdeath. 
One sitting nude they probably painted with Modigliani together - the paintings show the same shapes, the model's body has the 
same folds on her abdomen, the same painted hands, the same modeled shapes - which are elements quite different from other 
Modigliani paintings, including nudes that are flatter - rather areas lined with contour lines. However, Kisling's nude is still more 
modeled, with a smooth porcelain surface. Modigliani's painting, despite approaching his friend's style of painting, retained a 
certain unforced spontaneity.

women to cut their hair, which was previously completely inappropriate, as long hair was generally a sign of women. Towards the 
end of the second decade of the 20th century, a novelty began to spread in
