Description 
The painting being examined depicts a seated female nude sitting on a 
reddish pillow with greenish tassel trim in a darkened room. The seated 
female figure has her legs crossed and both arms joined below her left knee. 
The whole scene is set in the interior of the room, which is painted in dark 
shades of brown, ochre and green. A still life with a table and chair is painted 
in the upper right corner. There is a partial white tablecloth visible. On the left 
side lower edge is a reddish-brown drape. In the upper left side leading into 
the top center is what appears to be a wooden cabinet with a bowl and a gray 
looking cover on top of the cabinet. In the lower right corner is the signature 
“modigliani” in black paint. 
Research Methodology 
This examination was carried out based on requirement to verify the 
authenticity and style of the artwork in terms of technological and artistic 
quality. This work of art has already been the subject of several exhaustive 
technological analyses in the past (2011-Rome, 2014-Florence – see Exhibits), 
focusing mainly on its material composition, technical condition and 
subsequent restoration interventions. The subject of this study is therefore 
also a comparison of all previous results and their complementation with new 
findings and analyses. This work also includes a comparison of the artist's 
painting style, technique, materials, and signature. As part of the research, 
detailed photo-documentation of the front and back of the painting in visible 
daylight was carried out, as well as macro and micro images and photographs 
in bright side lighting (raking light). A verification image of Infrared 
Reflectography and Ultraviolet Luminescence was also taken. 
This work also includes X-Ray images using InsightART's RToo scanning 
technology. Drawn from CERN technology and used by NASA, the RToo 
provides pseudo-color X-ray scans, revealing differences in material 
compositions. Additional information is included in exhibits to this report. 
Results from previous scans and testing are compared and commented upon 
in this report.

Signature - Radiographic Analysis and Comparison: 
The signature was extensively examined for the purpose of its authenticity and belonging to the layers of painting itself. A 
survey of the material composition of the individual letters of the signature was carried out, which confirmed the presence 
of ivory black. The possibility of the occurrence of bone black with respect to trace amounts of other detected elements is 
also mentioned. Furthermore, the method of craquelure through individual letters of the signature was examined using a 
microscope. 
A radiographic analysis of the signatures was also performed and its comparison with other signatures from Modigliani's well- 
known works. See 2022 letter to the founder of InsightART, Jiri Lauterkranc and their Testing Report. The results of this 
survey show that there is a strong agreement on the basic characteristics of Modigliani's signature style. Based on these 
aspects, and similar data from 2011 and 2014 it can be stated that the signature is genuine.

Confirming Modigliani  Painting Style & Unique Techniques 
The most interesting discovery followed an investigation of the under-
painting, i. e. how the artist prepared his composition. They discovered that 
details of the face, nose, mouth, and eyes were uniformly drawn in “large and 
violent” rings of brown paint. These circular brushworks were so unvarying 
that this was a conclusive way to identify Modigliani’s hand. Genuine works 
always showed this artistic signature; faked works never did.  
The reader can see these brown circles are on this painting! That is as good as 
the signature of Modigliani being confirmed by three testing services as 
original and in “bone black” pigment and signed at the painting completion. 
Other details supporting this painting as a “style” by Modigliani are the lines 
and the body parts as discussed in items 9,10, and 11. This is yet another 
“atypical, but genuine” painting by Modigliani, similar to the Diego Rivera that 
was confirmed using the same analysis data.
