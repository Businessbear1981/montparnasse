## Sean's creative direction — what was set, by whom, on this date

The following calls were made by Sean during the working session of 29 May 2026 and are recorded here so the authorship trail is clear when the work moves forward:

1. **The book's central spine is Kiki Prin's life, told in parallel with Richard's four-year dig.** Not Richard's memoir with period vignettes — a co-equal biography with Richard as the doorway in.
2. **The narrator is Richard Triberg**, written in the voice of a man unguarded after four years of work — Sean's specific direction was for an interior register that combines the intimate first-person of *Catcher in the Rye*, the propulsive on-the-edge energy of Hunter S. Thompson, the global-chase action shape of *Indiana Jones* and *The Da Vinci Code*, with Hemingway present as a historical character and Fitzgerald used as period atmospheric wallpaper for the 1920s scenes.
3. **The painting at the centre is** Modigliani's *Sitting Nude with Crossed Hands*, c. 1917, 40.5 × 33 cm, currently at Institut Restellini Geneva, Mission Order signed 4 March 2026, financially responsible party Richard Triberg.
4. **The dual-timeline structure** — Sean's call: every chapter holds Richard in the present and Kiki in the past, separated and joined by specific verifiable facts about the canvas.
5. **The film director storyline** — Sean named the existence of a period film director who exploited Kiki in uncredited short films at fifteen-sixteen, per Richard's own research. Treatment to be confirmed with Richard before publication.
6. **Modigliani as obsessed-from-afar muse** — Sean's call. The 1917 relationship reframed: Modi watching her for two years from across the Rotonde, then a summer night in July with rain, wine, and the one formal sitting.
7. **Bill's package** — Sean's call. The mentor dies; Richard inherits the work via a small parcel wrapped in brown paper, handed to him by Bill's widow the morning after the funeral, with three words written by hand: *For Richard — find her.*
8. **Camille Aubry as the buddy** — Sean's call. A fictional senior scientist at Institut Restellini's Geneva lab. Composite character. The buddy-cop partner to Richard. Lives modern-timeline only. The book's final phone call is hers.
9. **Kiki as the Madonna of her time** — Sean's framing for the reader. The Lady Gaga. The Elizabeth Taylor. Not a footnote — the centre of the room. Used to ground readers who have not heard her name.
10. **Working title: Montparnasse.** Single word. No subtitle. Sean's call.
